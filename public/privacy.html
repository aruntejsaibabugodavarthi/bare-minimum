Privacy Policy

Compliant with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023.

1. Notice of Data Collection

Welcome to Bare Minimum. We collect and process your personal data solely to fulfill your orders, provide customer support, and improve your shopping experience. By using our services, you consent to our data practices as outlined below.

2. Types of Data We Collect

  • Identity & Contact Data: Name, phone number (OTP), email address, and shipping/billing addresses.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments (processed securely via our payment gateway) and the products you have purchased from us.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and interaction data via essential cookies.

3. Purpose of Processing

We process your data exclusively for:

  • Order fulfillment and delivery.
  • Legal and regulatory compliance (e.g., GST invoicing).
  • Account management and authentication.

4. Your Rights under the DPDP Act 2023

As a Data Principal, you have the right to:

  • Access & Summary: Request a summary of your personal data being processed.
  • Correction & Erasure: Request the correction of inaccurate data or the complete erasure of your personal data (Right to be Forgotten). You can permanently delete your account and data directly from your account settings.
  • Grievance Redressal: Register a grievance with our designated Data Protection Officer.

5. Data Retention & Erasure

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. If you exercise your Right to Erasure, we will permanently delete your account data, except where retention is legally mandated (e.g., completed financial transaction records for tax compliance).
